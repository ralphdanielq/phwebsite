TOURIST ATTRACTIONS IN LUZON





Intramuros





The walled city of Intramuros, located in the center of Manila City, is one of the most important historical sites in Manila and Luzon on the list. This Luzon tourism destination is one of the most accessible, affordable, and lovely locations in the area.

>

While you may explore Intramuros on foot and learn about it on your own, you can also join one of the many Intramuros tours to help you better understand the history that lies behind its walls. Take a look back at the history of Intramuros' Spanish colonial legacy while traveling about in a kalesa or horse-drawn carriage. You may also arrange for an Intramuros Manila bamboo bike tour.


You may travel by road, air, or sea to Puerto Princesa from Manila. After that, you'll travel via land transfers to Sabang. You are only a short distance from the exquisite allure of the Underground River once you arrive at Sabang Wharf.



Mayon Volcano





The mythical Mayon Volcano, sometimes known as Mount Mayon, is situated in the Bicol region's Albay province. The 2,462-meter-high volcano may be seen from Legazpi City and other Albay tourist destinations.


You will already be exposed to the splendor of this must-see Bicol tourist attraction as you arrive at the Bicol International Airport. If you join one of the Mayon Volcano excursions, you may climb up the lava wall, drive over difficult and muddy terrain, and take in the beautiful panorama while standing near to the volcano's foot for an even greater perspective. ATV tours in Bicol and Albay are a must-do when visiting this region of Luzon.


You may travel by road, air, or sea to Puerto Princesa from Manila. After that, you'll travel via land transfers to Sabang. You are only a short distance from the exquisite allure of the Underground River once you arrive at Sabang Wharf.





Puerto Princesa Underground River





The Subterranean River of Puerto Princesa, also known as the Puerto Princesa Underground River, is one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Additionally, it consistently appears on many tourists' bucket lists.


The 8.2 km long Puerto Princesa Underground River is famous for its crystal clear waters, breathtaking limestone cliffs, and cave formations. You may cruise here in a paddleboat and take in the breathtaking scenery. When you enter the caverns, you' ll encounter stalactites, stalagmites, birds, bats, and fish that live in the ecology of the Underground River.


Join a Puerto Princesa Underground River trip to discover this stunning, amazing beauty. Because there is a huge queue of people waiting to see this popular tourist destination in Palawan, Puerto Princesa excursions should be reserved well in advance. Additionally, it is strongly advised to plan your vacation for the dry season and to routinely check weather reports.


You may travel by road, air, or sea to Puerto Princesa from Manila. After that, you'll travel via land transfers to Sabang. You are only a short distance from the exquisite allure of the Underground River once you arrive at Sabang Wharf.
