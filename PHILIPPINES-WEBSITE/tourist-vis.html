TOURIST ATTRACTIONS IN VISAYAS





White Beach Boracay (Aklan)





One of the biggest tourist destinations in Boracay is White Beach, which is also the most well-known beach in the Visayas. It is 4 kilometers long and has crystal-clear seas and very fine white sand. The world is aware of Boracay because of White Beach, which is truly breathtaking.


WThe primary location for lodging, dining, and entertainment for visitors to Boracay is this beach. Since White Beach is home to the bulk of Boracay resorts, including Boracay hotels for families, it is a popular option for couples planning their Philippine honeymoon. Additionally, there are several Boracay eateries, caf√©s, bars, and boutiques lining this stretch of beach.




Chocolate Hills (Bohol)





One of the well-liked tourist destinations in Bohol is the Chocolate Hills. This location is a well-known tourist destination in the Visayas and is frequently cited as one of the world's natural marvels. The sheer quantity, symmetry, and color of these hills make them amazing.


Joining Chocolate Hills excursions will allow you to take in the area's more than 1700 hills, which are dispersed throughout a 50 square kilometer geographic area. These slopes are frequently covered in green grass. The hills, however, acquire their renowned chocolate brown hue during the dry season when the grass becomes dark.


Although the hills are varying sizes, they seem to be fairly symmetrical from a distance. The amazing beauty of the location is enhanced by its symmetry. Due to their perfect forms, they appeared artificial.





Kalanggaman Island (Leyte)





Due to its picturesque sandbars, clear sea, and powdery white sand, Kalanggaman Island is frequently referred to as "The Crown Jewel of Leyte" and is one of the top island-hopping tourist destinations in the Philippines. Kalanggaman has remained deserted for more than 750 meters. One of the longest sandbars in the nation surrounds it and is its most noticeable feature.


Travelers that take Kalanggaman Island excursions adore the atmosphere since it is peaceful, secure, and comforting. It is neither very developed, nor is it frequently overcrowded. You may unwind here in the shade of the palm palms while taking in the breathtaking Visayan scenery. On your trip to this island, you may also partake in sports like kayaking, scuba diving, and snorkeling if you want to be more active.


You may travel by road, air, or sea to Puerto Princesa from Manila. After that, you'll travel via land transfers to Sabang. You are only a short distance from the exquisite allure of the Underground River once you arrive at Sabang Wharf.
